Welcome to NTDGAMES
This is a personal project where I learn and practice game development. I'm not a professional developer-just someone who enjoys games and wants to create them on my own.
The projects here are mostly small experiments made during my learning process, covering programming, design, and game structure. Each project reflects what I'm learning at the time.
As I gain more experience, I plan to work on more complete and polished projects in the future.
Thank you for taking the time to visit.
