Dark Maze

Last Update: 2025-12-18

Lost in a dark and vast maze, you have only a flashlight to find your way out. The game is a race against time, where you must strategically manage your Stamina to sprint or walk to recover. Watch the Minimap to find the glowing white pillar marking the only exit at the edge of the map. Every second is precisely calculated, challenging you to break your own records in this dark and breathless atmosphere.
